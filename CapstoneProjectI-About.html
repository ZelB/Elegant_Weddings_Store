ABOUT US

COMPANY BACKGROUND


Elegant Weddings Décor Shop was founded in January 2021.

A team of creative designers was formed to attend to any bridal party’s needs. Elegant Weddings Décor Shop strives to deliver the highest quality of service, on time, every time.

Elegant Weddings Décor Shop provides all your wedding decoration needs. From the Church to the reception. We believe everybody is unique and that is why we also offer custom made packages.




SERVICES


Elegant Weddings Décor Shop has a dedicated team which provides the following personalized services:

  • Bridal Bouquets
  • Bride and Groom Chair Decorations
  • Church Chair Decoration
  • Confetti and Holder
  • Door Decoration
  • Farm Table Theme Decoration
  • Guest Book
  • Guest Gift Sets
